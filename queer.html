Projects About Visual diary

As a photographer and maker I research queer nightlife/lifestyle. I personally connect to this topic, and when I moved to Rotterdam I felt the need to connect more with my queer identity. I did this by applying for a job at Ferry Rotterdam and documenting my journey there.

In this project I either isolate subjects in the studio, or shoot on location to give context to the images. The goal is to showcase queer joy and highlight acceptance rather than tolerance.

As a maker I appreciate the art of drag and love capturing it. The magical atmosphere is something I try and convey through imagery.
